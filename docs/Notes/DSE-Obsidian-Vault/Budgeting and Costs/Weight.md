The plane has to be at most 50 kg in weight, that means that the structure has to be as  light as possible. 
-out of the 50 kg, we have at least 5 kg for the payload, consisting of sensors and actuators

-the engines are 2.1 kg a piece and we might have to use 2 engines ---> 4.2 kg for the engines + piping and wires this will add to roughly 5 kg  (to be determined at a later stage the exact components and weight)

1. Material used for fuselage design and dimensions
2. Wing dimensions and materials used (this can be expanded depending on the internal structure and resistance of the wing, mission scope influences a lot this part)
3. Landing gear 
4. Payload (sensor and actuators)
